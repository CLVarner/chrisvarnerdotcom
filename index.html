Chris Varner has been with WebFX for 6 years and moved from direct tactical delivery of marketing campaigns, including SEO and PPC, to a forward-leadership role to prepare the future of WebFX’s 700 strong workforce for the next phase of marketing.

Prior to working at WebFX, Chris held various roles including being an independent contractor responsible for both sales and marketing, as well as leading change in the community development and non-profit spaces.

Generative AI. ChatGPT. Agents. Everyday something changes that directly impacts the marketing role. While the potential in these new tools is fascinating, the largest and most chaotic change is how people’s behavior adapts to the new world.
